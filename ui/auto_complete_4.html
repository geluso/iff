# Sidewalk
You are standing on the doormat locked outside your humble apartment. You have on a thin jacket, pants, shirt, and shoes. On the ground is a piece of gum. It's not enough protection from the cold. A feeble flickering street lamp shines meager light on vicinity.

> You check under the doormat.

That's where you store your spare key. It's missing.

> You inspect your jacket.

Your favorite jacket. It's awesome. It might not look awesome, but looks aren't everything. It's what's inside that counts. Inside you find a lump of heavy cheese.
